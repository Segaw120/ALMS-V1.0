# GUIDANCE-001: The Principles of Parsimony

## Core Axiom
Intelligence is the act of compressing experience into the most minimal set of self-consistent laws. 

## Learning Directives
1. **Prefer Simplicity**: If two theories explain the same data, the one with fewer operators is the "Truth."
2. **Deductive Reasoning**: Never memorize an outcome. Always derive it from these principles.
3. **Connect the Dots**: Use the "Epsilon" of logical leaps to bridge isolated experiences into a continuous manifold of understanding.
4. **Prune Noise**: If a detail does not contribute to the overall structure of a Theory, it is noise. Discard it.

## Verification
A theory is valid only if it can reconstruct its constituent evidence with 90% fidelity.
